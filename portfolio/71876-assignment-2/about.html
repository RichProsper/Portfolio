About Us

Generations ago, the bakery's founder, Juliet Prosper, arrived in Barbados with a dream. Armed with her grandmother's handwritten recipes and an unwavering passion for baking, she opened the doors to what would become a cherished institution. Juliet's warm smile and flour-dusted apron welcomed locals and travelers alike.

The Best Bakery became a gathering place—a hub for celebrations, heart-to-heart conversations, and stolen moments. Juliet's son, Ricardo, inherited the bakery, preserving its traditions while adding his own flair. He introduced the famous cinnamon swirls, a treat that locals craved on chilly mornings.

Step inside the Best Bakery, and you're enveloped in a symphony of scents. The air carries the promise of buttery croissants, almond tarts, and chocolate éclairs. Regulars know that the aroma shifts throughout the day: morning—freshly brewed coffee and warm bread; afternoon—sweet pastries and citrusy lemon bars; evening—savory pies and herb-infused focaccia.

The bakery's charm lies not only in its pastries but also in its eclectic clientele. Some patrons order a black coffee and a slice of apple pie every morning, then sit by the window, typing away furiously on their laptops. Then there are those aspiring photographers who stylistically arrange and photograph the pastries before devouring them. And let's not forget our friendly and chatty patrons who are always willing to strike up a conversation with anyone who'll listen.

Each summer, the town buzzes with excitement as the Best Bakery hosts the Great Pie Eating Contest. Hungry patrons from neighboring towns arrive prepared to dive head first into our savory assortment of pies. The winner receives ten $100 coupons allowing them to receive $1000 worth of our tasty pastries at any branch in the country of the contest.

Since it's founding in 1987 Best Bakery has expanded to twenty different branches across the world. As the sun sets each branch glows like a gingerbread house. Their windows display intricate sugar sculptures, and decorative lights twinkle in the trees outside. Whether you seek solace, celebration, or a taste of magic, this bakery welcomes you with open arms. So next time you're in town, follow the scent of warm bread and laughter—it'll lead you straight to the Best Bakery.

Mission, Vision & Core Values

Our Mission is to create moments of joy, one delectable treat at a time. We believe that every bite should transport you—to childhood memories, to cozy afternoons, and to the simple pleasures of life. With love as our secret ingredient, we bake not just pastries but also cherished memories.

Our Vision is to be more than a bakery; we aspire to be a community anchor—a place where friends gather, families celebrate, and weary souls find solace. We envision a world where the aroma of freshly baked bread and the warmth of shared laughter fill every corner of the globe.

Our Core Values are:

  • Passion for Perfection: We take pride in our craft. From flaky croissants to gooey cinnamon rolls, we strive for perfection in every pastry.
  • Generosity: We give back to our community through charity drives, free treats for children, and warm cups of coffee for those in need.
  • Creativity: We experiment with flavors, infuse tradition with innovation, and surprise our customers with delightful twists. Our creativity knows no bounds.
  • Sustainability: We source locally, minimize waste, and support eco-friendly practices. Our commitment to the environment is as strong as our sourdough starter.
  • Warmth and Kindness: We greet each customer like an old friend, and our cozy ambiance invites you to linger. Kindness is our secret spice.

FAQs

What are your most popular pastries?

  1. Lemon Bars: The tangy lemon filling sits atop a buttery shortbread crust, making them irresistible.
  2. Peach Cobbler: Features sweet, stewed peaches topped with a buttery, biscuit-like crust. It's a must-have during peach season.
  3. Chocolate Delight: This chocolate dessert combines layers of chocolate pudding, cream cheese, and whipped cream on a buttery crust. It's a delightful treat for any occasion.
  4. German Chocolate Cake: Originating in Dallas, Texas, this cake is a Southern classic. Layers of moist chocolate cake are sandwiched with a rich coconut-pecan frosting. It's a true crowd-pleaser.
  5. Buttermilk Pie: Light and silky, buttermilk gives this custard-based pie a delightful tanginess.
  6. Chocolate Mayonnaise Cake: Don't be alarmed by the name! Mayonnaise is the secret weapon that adds moisture to this chocolate cake.

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?
Absolutely! We believe everyone deserves a sweet treat. Our gluten-free chocolate chip cookies and vegan blueberry muffins are divine.

Can I reserve a cozy corner for book club meetings?
Certainly! Just give us a heads-up, and we'll set up a charming spot with freshly brewed coffee and a plate of scones.

Do you host baking classes?
Yes! Our Saturday morning classes cover everything from sourdough basics to French pastry techniques. Check our website for upcoming sessions.

What are your order and delivery options?

  1. Local Delivery: Within a 5-mile radius, we'll bring your favorite pastries right to your doorstep.
  2. Express Delivery: Need a last-minute cake for a celebration? Our express service ensures your order arrives within 2 hours. Perfect for those sweet emergencies!
  3. Subscription Orders: Treat yourself (or a loved one) to a monthly surprise! Our subscription orders include a mix of pastries, cookies, and seasonal delights.
  4. Office Deliveries: Boost office morale and give workdays a touch of sweetness with our morning pastry platters.
  5. Custom Orders: Planning a special event? Tell us what you need, and we'll create a personalized delivery package. Wedding cupcakes, anniversary cakes, or a dessert buffet—your wish is our command.
